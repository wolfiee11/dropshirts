The Story

My name is Anaf Tyfin Khan. I’m 14 years old, and I started DropShirts because I wanted to create something that didn't exist in my local market: a truly premium, heavyweight essential t-shirt.

Living in Bangladesh, we are surrounded by garment manufacturing, yet finding that perfect boxy, thick-fabric fit is harder than it looks. I spent weeks researching GSM, sourcing the right cotton, and designing a minimalist look that speaks for itself.

I’m starting this journey by selling to my inner circle—my friends. They are my first models and my biggest critics. When you receive a DropShirts package in our signature black matte mailer, know that it was packed by a 14-year-old with a vision to change the local streetwear game.

— Anaf Tyfin Khan, Founder
